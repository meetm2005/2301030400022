Frequently Asked Questions
What is VR History?
VR History provides immersive virtual reality experiences to learn about historical events interactively.
What devices are supported?
Our platform supports most VR headsets, including Oculus, HTC Vive, and Windows Mixed Reality devices.
How can I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the 'Forgot Password' link to receive reset instructions.
Who can I contact for billing issues?
You can contact our billing support team by emailing billing@vrhistory.com.
Are new historical events added regularly?
Yes, we regularly update our platform with new historical events to enhance your learning experience.
Is VR History suitable for educational institutions?
Absolutely! Our platform is designed for both individual users and classrooms, providing an engaging learning tool for students.
Can I use VR History without a VR headset?
Yes, while the best experience is through a VR headset, you can access content on desktop or mobile devices in 2D mode.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for new users to explore our platform and content.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other standard payment methods.
How can I provide feedback on the platform?
You can provide feedback using the form on our Contact Us page or by emailing support@vrhistory.com.
